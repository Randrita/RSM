About us

About RSM

RSM is an Online Music Institute that aims to redefine online music teaching and learning.We provide one-on-one sessions by professional faculties, with specialism in performance and musical theory. We also offer a range of courses in western classical music catering to different proficiency levels and preferences. We believe that every individual has their own objective behind endeavoring their musical journey, hence the sessions offered by us are entirely redesigned on case basis so that it’s aligned to each individual’s aspirations, age and learning pace.
